Skill Resources

Bharat Skills

Bharat Skills is a national portal for providing information and resources for skill development in India. It aims to create a skilled workforce that can contribute to India's economic growth and development. The portal provides information on various skill development initiatives, training programs, and job opportunities in different sectors. It also offers a platform for employers to connect with skilled workers and for individuals to showcase their skills and find job opportunities.

Visit Bharat Skills

PSSCIVE

The Pandit Sundarlal Sharma Central Institute of Vocational Education (PSSCIVE) is a national institute for promoting and developing vocational education and training in India. It offers various vocational training programs, research and development activities, and consultancy services. The institute aims to create a skilled workforce that is globally competitive and can contribute to the socio-economic development of the country.

Visit PSSCIVE

ESkill India

ESkill India is an initiative of the Government of India to provide free online skill development courses to Indian citizens. The platform offers courses in various domains such as IT, banking, retail, hospitality, and many more. It aims to provide skill development opportunities to individuals who cannot afford traditional training programs and help them secure better job opportunities.

Visit ESkill India
